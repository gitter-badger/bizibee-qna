Features

Participants can practice their business and decision-making skills with a state of the art online team environment.

Strategic Thinking

Experience the challenges of strategic competition in multi-sided markets.
Decision Making

Immediately receive feedback about the impact of decisions.
Problem Solving

The right decisions lead to success while the wrong ones to invaluable problem solving experiences.
Flexible, Customizable Platform

Adapts easily to a variety of formats (virtual and classroom learning, individual and group experiences, online and paper-based options).
Options for Presenting Results

Enables participants to see individual results as well as team results.
Broad Range of Decision Structures

Enables a wide variety of simulation decisions, including unprompted, open-ended decisions that encourage participants to think out of the box.
